Read the Quick Facts Breadcrumb Home Hampton Roads Naval ROTC Hampton Roads Naval ROTC Welcome to one of the nation's largest ROTC units 170+ Sailors/ Marines/ Midshipmen 5 Participating Schools The Hampton Roads Naval Reserve Officer Training Corps is one of the largest ROTC Units in the nation, consisting of more than 170 Sailors, Marines, and Midshipmen. Our location, close to the world's largest naval base, allows us to offer our students unparalleled leadership and hands-on training opportunities. The Hampton Roads Consortium is comprised of a six schools that include Old Dominion University, Norfolk State University, Hampton University, Regent University, Virginia Wesleyan University, and Tidewater Community College.
