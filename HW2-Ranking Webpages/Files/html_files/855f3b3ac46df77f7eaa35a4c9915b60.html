At Old Dominion University, our diverse community is empowered to thrive in a charged atmosphere that is both inclusive and competitive. The ODU Recreation and Wellness department delivers superb programs, facilities and services for students, faculty, staff, and friends to promote healthy and active lifestyles that allow our Monarchs to live and learn well. Student Recreation Center Whether you are a serious athlete or just love working up a good sweat, ODU provides facilities and events for everyone to enjoy and participate in physical activity.
