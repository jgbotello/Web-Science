Financial aid options Through various programs, we help admitted students who qualify for additional financial resources attend RISD and benefit from a world-class education. Aid for undergraduates is available through scholarships, federal and state grants, need-based loans and work study opportunities. All admitted graduate students are additionally considered for fellowships and assistantships. These provide both financial support and important professional development experiences.
