College Advising Centers | Old Dominion University Skip to main content Apply Visit Give myODU Menu Search Close Breadcrumb Home Academic Advising College Advising Centers College Advising Centers At ODU we utilize professional advisors and faculty advisors to support our diverse student population. Our university policy requires all degree seeking students to meet with their advisor to ensure academic success. Degree plan holds which impact registration, become effective either mid-October for the following Spring and Summer registration, or mid-March for the following Fall registration period. To find help you locate your advisor please select the college below in which you have chosen to major within. All newly admitted freshmen are required to attend orientation. During orientation your Degree Plan hold will be lifted, you will meet with your advisor and enroll in classes. If you have questions about Monarch Orientation, please contact orientation@odu.edu. Newly Admitted Transfer Students Transfers with 23 or fewer transfer credits are required to participate in Monarch Orientation. Please register for Monarch Orientation, and if you have questions or concerns contact orientation@odu.edu. Transfer students with 24 or more transfer credits may participate in Monarch Orientation OR connect directly with the Transfer Success Advisor or the College Advising Center directly for assistance. Please connect with the center using your ODU email address and be prepared to provide your university identification number. Continuing Undergraduate Students Please connect with the College Advising Center via email for assistance or you can schedule with your assigned academic advisor by logging into your MyODU Portal and utilizing your “Student Success Services” tool. Appointments are highly recommended! Once you meet with your advisor, your Degree Plan hold will be removed for enrollment. Second Degree Seeking Student If you have completed your admissions process for Second Degree Seeking status, please connect directly with the College Advising Center via email for assistance. Please connect with the center using your ODU email address and be prepared to provide your university identification number. Non-Degree Seeking Students If you have completed your admissions process for Non-Degree Seeking status, please connect directly with the Center for Advising Administration and Academic partnerships for assistance, advisor@odu.edu. Please connect with the center using your ODU email address and be prepared to provide your university identification number. Graduate Students Graduate Students should connect directly with their Graduate Program Director (GPD) or graduate advisor for help with holds, advising, and course registration. Your GPD should have access to lift the degree plan hold. If you are not sure who to contact, you may search for your academic program or connect directly with The Graduate School.  Strome College of Business Undergraduate Advising Center 1011 Constant Hall Email: BusinessAdvising@odu.edu Phone: 757-683-5777 Student Type: Newly Admitted Freshmen If you have never attended ODU previously, then please register for Monarch Orientation. Graduate Students Graduate Students should connect directly with their Graduate Program Director (GPD) or graduate advisor for help with holds, advising, and course registration. Your GPD should have access to lift the degree plan hold. If you are not sure who to contact, you may search for your academic program or connect directly with The Graduate School.
