Or, it can be quiet, reflective, and studious. How you plug into ODU's electrified atmosphere is up to you. Experience at ODU spans borders, languages and time. Cultures converge. Viewpoints are respected. On one of the most diverse campuses in Virginia, there is no end to the fascinating mix of stories you'll hear, people you'll meet, and the interests you'll share.
