Our staff can connect reporters with faculty experts, assist news crews with filming on campus, arrange video and radio feeds, or help members of the media locate archived photos. Public Relations Tools and Resources All media inquiries about stories or events related to research, arts, faculty, student and staff at Old Dominion should be directed to the Public Affairs & News Bureau by calling 757-683-3152. For a story or event that involves the Athletics Department, you may wish to call 757-683-3375. Journalists on deadline may also contact Public Affairs staff directly.
