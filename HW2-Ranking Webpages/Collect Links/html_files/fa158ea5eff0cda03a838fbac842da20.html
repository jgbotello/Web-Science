We cannot fulfill e-mail or telephone requests for transcripts. Transcripts for work at other institutions, including high schools, become the property of Old Dominion University and will not be released to third parties. See Holds for information on holds that may prevent the student from obtaining an official or unofficial transcript. To check on the progress of your transcript request, login to your Parchment account. For general questions related to transcripts email transcripts@odu.edu. Transcript Fees Payment is required before transcripts will be sent. $5.00 for each official transcript requested. Orders placed through Parchment will incur an additional fee. No charge for unofficial transcripts.   Payment Options   In person at the Rollins Hall Cashier's Office (Cash or Check only). Credit card information provided on requests made to Parchment. Requesting Transcripts Requesting an OFFICIAL transcript: Parchment (electronic PDF/hardcopy) (additional fees apply) Delivery tracking included. Hardcopy orders submitted by 8pm Eastern time will be sent the same day. Electronic orders are typically sent within 24 hours. Up to 16 pages of attachments can be added to either electronic or hardcopy transcripts for no additional charge. (Parchment option NOT AVAILABLE for students who attended prior to 1982 or Clinical Psychology Consortium students)   Recent graduates: Please be aware that your transcript will not show your degree information until your degree has been awarded, which can take up to 6 weeks from the end of the semester. You can check your degree status in Leo Online by going to "Admissions, Registration, Student Records, Graduation Information", clicking on "Graduation" and "View Graduation Status". If you need to show proof of degrees earned, you should place your order only after your degree has been awarded. In Person (hardcopy only) (cash or check payment). Requesting an UNOFFICIAL transcript: LEO Online (hardcopy only) In Person (hardcopy only)   Online Requests Electronic (PDF) and hardcopy transcripts are now fulfilled through Parchment. Students may still use the online request in LEO Online to order a printed UNOFFICIAL transcript. MIDAS ID and password are required to log into LEO Online. Holds No transcript will be released if the student has financial or academic holds. Please make sure you do not have any holds on your account by checking the View Holds link in LEO Online. (The Degree Plan hold does not prevent transcript requests.) Orders placed through Parchment will be checked for holds, and the requestor will be notified at the time of the order if the order cannot be fulfilled due to holds. If the hold is resolved within 30 days, the transcript will be released and the requestor will be charged. Otherwise, the order will be cancelled and the requestor will not be charged. Leo Online Parchment Obtaining Transcripts Official Transcripts - Electronic (PDF) or Printed Unofficial Transcripts Recipients of electronic (PDF) transcripts are sent a link via e-mail when the transcript is available to be retrieved. This link can only be used one time and expires after 30 days. The requestor will be notified via email and, if applicable, by text message to your mobile device when the transcript has been retrieved (or if the recipient has NOT retrieved the transcript). Official printed transcripts are always mailed (or picked up by the requester) and are only official if in the sealed Old Dominion University envelope. Transcripts issued to the student or picked up at the Registrar's Office, will be stamped "Issued to Student". The records of individuals who attended Old Dominion University prior to 1982 may not be computerized. If your record is not found you can still order a transcript, and the order will be researched and fulfilled manually by the Registrar's Office. Additional time should be allowed for the creation of those records (this may take up to 10 business days). To view your unofficial transcript: Log in to LEO Online secure area (MIDAS ID and password required) In LEO Online secure area, select Admission, Registration, Student Records, Graduation Information, Credit Card Payments, and Financial Aid. Select Student Records. Select Academic Transcript and follow the prompts. If you do not know your MIDAS ID and password, you can retrieve/reset them at http://midas.odu.edu. You will need your University ID number (UIN). If you do not know your UIN, in most cases you can retrieve it at http://midas.odu.edu ("Forgot UIN?"), or you may contact register@odu.eduwith your full name and date of birth. For assistance with your MIDAS account, please contact itshelp@odu.edu or call 757-683-3192. Unofficial transcripts are available for viewing on the secure web site at LEO Online and are intended for personal use only. On an exceptional basis, with written and signed request, an unofficial transcript can be faxed to a third party. An official transcript must be requested at the same time and will be mailed to the same third party. Unofficial transcripts are NOT available from Parchment but may be requested through LEO Online.
