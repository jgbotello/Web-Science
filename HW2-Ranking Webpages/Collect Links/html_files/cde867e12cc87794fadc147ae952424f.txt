            Attention
          


Cash is no longer accepted for transactions at Monarch Card & Business Services. You can pay using Debit, Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and/or American Express. Thank you for understanding.





















            Mission
          


The mission of Monarch Card & Business Services is to improve the quality and convenience of ODU campus life. Convenience, simplicity, access and ensuring security -- one card for all your needs!
Monarch Card & Business Services manages identification card production, security, and card technology for all students, faculty, and staff throughout the Old Dominion University campus community.

            Student ID
      









            Faculty/Staff ID
      











Your ID card is your official University identification card and access credentials for the campus. Please do not put holes in your ID card because the holes will make the card inoperable. There is a $15 charge for a replacement.
What does the $4 fee cover?
The fee covers new Smart Card Technology that increases card security and campus safety.







































            Where can I use it?
      


Your new ID card is a multi-functioning campus ID. It allows for access and services throughout the campus and should be carried on you at all times.

New Feature: PNC Bank account linking










PNC is making it easier for you to keep track of your money at Old Dominion University. You'll find a PNC branch in the North Mall of the Webb Center and seven PNC ATMs on campus. And with PNC Virtual Wallet Student®, managing your money is easier too.











Image




















As an ODU University student and PNC customer, you can link your bank account to your Monarch ID Card and use it at PNC ATMs and other merchants. You can even request a PNC Bank Visa ® debit card designed just for Old Dominion University. For more information, click here.

Monarch Plus Account:

This account serves as a University prepaid debit account that serves to make purchases at many on-campus locations, including all dining locations, select vending machines, select printers, the University Bookstore, Monarch Techstore, the Health Center, and more.
Monarch Plus purchases can be made at select off-campus locations.

Meal Plans and Flex Points:

Meal Plans are ODU's way for students to purchase food on campus, per semester.
Flex Points are tax-free points to be used at select Monarch Dining locations.
