Undergraduate 




Apply as an undergraduate if you want to enroll in:A bachelor's degree programAn undergraduate certificate programIndividual credit-bearing courses at the undergraduate levelFuture Monarch PortalIf you experience technical difficulties in the portal, please email globaladmissions@odu.edu for support with your application, admission, or confirmation.
 



 





 
 

 




 
Graduate 




Apply as a graduate if you want to enroll in: A master's, education specialist, or doctoral degree programA graduate certificate programA teaching endorsement outside of a master's programIndividual credit-bearing courses at the graduate levelODU Application Portal
 



 





 
 

 




 
International 




If you want to pursue an undergraduate or a graduate online program from your home country outside of the United States, start here. ODU Application Portal
