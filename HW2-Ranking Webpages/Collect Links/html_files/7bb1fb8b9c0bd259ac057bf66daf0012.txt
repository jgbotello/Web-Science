 Accounts & Access




Information for access to ODU's computing applications and resources.





MIDAS & MonarchKey
MIDAS is our identity and password management system, giving you access to most of ODU's technical services. 

                    MIDAS & MonarchKey








Banner
Banner is our centralized records system, allowing approved users to view and manipulate data.

                    Banner

 Communication & Collaboration



ODU offers many ways for faculty, staff and students to work together to accomplish great things.





Faculty/Staff Email
Faculty & staff have Microsoft Exchange email accounts that can be accessed through Microsoft Outlook.

                    Faculty/Staff Email








Telecommunications
ODU's telecommunication systems include VOIP, cellular telephone service, voicemail, conference calls and more.

                    Telecommunications




 More Communication & Collaboration Tools








 Instructional Tools



Teaching faculty have access to many electronic tools to make connecting with their classes easier.





Canvas
As an instructor, you can use Canvas to post assignments and enable collaboration and feedback. 

                    Canvas








Top Hat
Engage students with interactive slides, graded questions, videos, discussions and polls.

                    Top Hat

Multi-Function Printers

Institutional Computer Purchases

 Remote Computing



Virtual Private Network and Remote Desktop Access enable faculty/staff to connect to ODU resources while working remotely.





Virtual Private Network (VPN)
The VPN provides an encrypted connection so users can interact with IT systems as if they were on campus.

                    Virtual Private Network (VPN)








Remote Teaching
As an instructor, you may need to teach a class remotely or make a class available for students to attend remotely. 

                    Remote Teaching
