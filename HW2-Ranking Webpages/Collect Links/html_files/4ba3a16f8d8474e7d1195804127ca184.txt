Institutional Effectiveness & Assessment










As part of ODU's commitment to continuous quality improvement designed to impact learning and teaching and meet accreditation standards, the Office of Institutional Effectiveness and Assessment (IE&A) collaborates with and supports leadership, faculty, and staff with evaluating and assessing activities and making evidence-based improvements.
