Your New to Blue: New Student Experience includes three unique phases throughout the summer that will outline your required orientation activities, provide you with an online orientation course, and prepare you to visit campus this summer. Make Me a Monarch Make Me a Monarch kicks off your new student experience at Old Dominion University. Following Admitted Students Day, we will have a weekly Q&A via Zoom to answer all your new student questions. Registration for Make Me A Monarch is open to new students who have paid their admissions deposit and set up their Microsoft 365 email account. Review the New to Blue timeline and checklist, and complete the tasks and to-do's to continue progressing through your new student experience.    Explore Your Kingdom New students will enroll in New Student Experience course and complete Explore Your Kingdom: Pre-Orientation module. The course will open following Admitted Students Day. Learn more Discover Your Pride New students will register for an on-campus Discover Your Pride: Monarch Orientation session. Registration will open following Admitted Students Day. View details Rule Your Dominion New students will return to the New Student Experience course and complete Rule Your Dominion: Post-Orientation module.
