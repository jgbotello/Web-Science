Learn more about the program here.  Dragas Family Scholarship Dragas Family Scholarship funds a first-year or sophomore, undergraduate, international applicant from Greece planning to study at Old Dominion University. International applicants are individuals intending to apply for an F-1 visa who hold citizenship in a country outside the U.S. and who don't also possess U.S. citizenship or permanent residency. Explore Opportunities Faculty Exchange: Making it Happen Can you teach without speaking the language? You certainly can. Click on the tabs below to explore your options. Japan Faculty Exchange Program UKK Faculty Exchange Program - Suspended Until Further Notice The University of Kitakyushu is a long-time ODU & Norfolk Sister Cities partner. The University of Kitakyushu has offered the opportunity to send one faculty member to teach an American society/American culture-related course to their students this summer. The program is open to full time faculty members from Humanities, Social Sciences and Liberal Arts. Click to learn more. China Faculty Exchange Program Henan University Program - Suspended Until Further Notice The Center for Global Engagement offers ODU faculty members serval teaching opportunities in China during the summer months. Faculty members teach 2 to 3-week courses in a variety of academic disciplines.  Henan University is one of the oldest and key universities in China. Click here to learn more about this program.     Beijing Wuzi University Program - Suspended Until Further Notice Beijing Wuzi University invites professors to teach entry/intermediate-level courses in the summer to undergraduate students. There is a demand for teaching experience in logistics, economics, management, and information systems. Beijing Wuzi University, a long-time ODU partner, places priority on international collaborations and is proactive in exploring academic exchanges with the outside world. Click here to learn more about this program.
