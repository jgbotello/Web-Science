Program HighlightsCareersCoursesRequirementsTransferCostNext-StepsContact Request Info Apply About this Program A Bachelor of Science Degree (BSME) in Mechanical Engineering is an accredited four-year program of study, which requires 128 credit hours. Our program prepares students for professional careers in all areas of engineering. It also prepares students for graduate study in mechanical engineering or related fields.
