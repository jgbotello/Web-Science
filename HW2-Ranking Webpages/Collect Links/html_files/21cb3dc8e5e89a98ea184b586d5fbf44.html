Computer Science Degree Program Offerings This program is offered at the following locations: Residential Campus Fayette, MO Online Programs Online Programs About Our Computer Science Degree Program  The Computer Science major will build your analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills as you learn to investigate how computer systems function. You will develop skills to prepare you for this ever-evolving field by taking classes focusing on programming, architecture, networks, databases, AI, algorithms, and computer security. As a CMU Computer Science major, you will have opportunities to: strengthen your critical thinking and analytical skills during hands-on classes with focuses in networking, application programming, computing systems, databases, and software packages develop a broad understanding of computer languages and build an in-demand skill set that prepares you for careers in business or industry evaluate and review systems, diagnose problems, and establish solutions request information!   Degrees Available Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science - Fayette Campus, Online / Statewide Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - Fayette Campus, Online / Statewide a minor in Computer Science is available - Fayette Campus, Online / Statewide Admission Requirements for Computer Science Majors Admission to CMU is selective.
