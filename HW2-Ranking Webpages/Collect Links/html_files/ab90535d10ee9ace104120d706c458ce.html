Requirements What are the requirements to apply for Health Education at ODU? Students entering the Minor program in Health Education should meet the minimum university admission requirements Undergraduate Admission. BIOL 240 or BIOL 250 and HPE 230 are prerequisites for the minor and are not included in the calculation of the grade point average for the minor. 12 total hours are required to complete this minor. Transfer Info Featured Courses HPE 317 View full course description for Human Growth & Motor Development This course is an examination of the physical growth and motor development of the human being over the life span.… HPE 317 Human Growth & Motor Development This course is an examination of the physical growth and motor development of the human being over the life span. Emphasis is on the assessment of physical and cognitive development, particularly in the K-12 ages. Theory and technique for research are discussed and the use of research findings is incorporated into the assessment materials. Attention is directed toward acquisition of basic skills, perceptual-motor development, and age-related changes. Prerequisites: HPE 200.
