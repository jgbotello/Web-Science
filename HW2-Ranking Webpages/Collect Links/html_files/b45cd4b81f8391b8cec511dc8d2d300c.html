Concept of Public Finance management you should understand Your university assignments are concerned about the management of public finance. Students are pursuing finance area well aware of the terminology and concepts of economics and finance. However, understanding the management of public funds is what public finance management all about. It includes mobilization of the resources, budgeting process, effective and efficient resource management, etc. Components of public finance Expectations of the people within a country is rising due to the changing times; hence the area of financial management has become more demanding and vital. Public finance consists of the public expenditure, public revenue, public debt, Federal administration and Federal finance. Government and public finance The government utilizes the concept of taxes in the form of Excise tax, Sales tax, Value added tax gift tax, duties and personal income tax to generate the revenue. The generated revenue is spent on building better infrastructure and providing facilities to the public. Other sources of government revenue are government-owned corporations, sales of assets and other funds. The Government sometimes takes a loan from the World Bank to support the operations in a country.
