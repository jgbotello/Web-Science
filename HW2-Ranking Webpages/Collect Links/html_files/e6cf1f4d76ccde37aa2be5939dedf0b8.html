University Strategic Communication & Marketing is the central authority and resource for campus communications and the University brand. Strategic Communication and Marketing works to position Old Dominion University as a forward-focused leader in research, innovation, teaching and community engagement; enhances the University's reputation and is the official producer of news about the University.
