Societal Marketing Concept Societal marketing is a concept that assists businesses in operating by marketing principles and customer needs. In this context, the customer's preferences are given the top spot. The organization following the societal marketing concepts is different from the usual business firms. Satisfying customers is their first priority, while profit is given the second spot. The concepts of societal marketing emerged in the late 1960s and early 1970s, but they only became popular in the twenty-first century. With these marketing concepts, the students learn how to make lifelong customers for their organization, which will generate lifelong profit. They mainly thicken their customer relationships, which will benefit both the customer and the organization in the long run.
