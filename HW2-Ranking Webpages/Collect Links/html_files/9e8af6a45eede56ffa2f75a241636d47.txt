License Plate Recognition





        ODU utilizes License Plate Recognition, a virtual permit system that allows the campus community to use their car's license plate as a parking permit.
            




Learn More

























Sustainable Transportation





        ODU encourages sustainable commuting habits by partnering to provide a variety of greener transportation options.
            




Learn More

























Tidal Flooding





        ODU's Norfolk campus is sometimes affected by Tidal Flooding, which is a type of flooding not caused by rain or storms, but by low elevation & natural tidal fluctuations.
            




Learn More

























Heads Up Monarchs





        The Heads Up Monarchs campaign encourages ODU Monarchs and community members traveling across campus on foot, bike, or by bus or car to keep their heads up and pay attention to their surroundings.
