Requirements What are the requirements to apply for Speech-Language Pathology & Audiology at ODU? Students entering the Minor program in Speech-Language Pathology & Audiology should meet the minimum university admission requirements Undergraduate Admission. For completion of a minor, a student must have a minimum overall cumulative grade point average of 2.00 in all courses specified as a requirement for the minor exclusive of prerequisite courses and complete a minimum of six hours in upper-level courses in the minor requirement through courses offered by Old Dominion University.
