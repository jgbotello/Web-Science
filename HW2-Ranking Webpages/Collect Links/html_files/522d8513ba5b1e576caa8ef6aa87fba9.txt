Old Dominion University (ODU) is a military-friendly institution with a quarter of the student body affiliated with the United States military. We provide an extensive list of services to help you succeed from application to graduation. Be sure to check out the recently renovated community lounge, study spaces, and cyber lounges for military-affiliated students in Monarch Hall. A contact list is down below. Please don't hesitate to reach out with questions.

Military Connection Center





Contact the Military Connection Center for assistance with applying to ODU, enrolling for classes, career counseling, and/or getting connected with campus.

Email: military@odu.edu
Phone: 757-683-7153








Military Distance Learning





Contact Dan Whiting for assistance with distance learning enrollment.

Email: dwhiting@odu.edu
Phone: 757-683-5783








Veterans Affairs Team





Contact the Registrar's Office Veterans Affairs Team for assistance with the G.I. Bill, Certifying classes through the VA, and other tuition benefit questions.

Email: vaservices@odu.edu
Phone: 757-683-4425
