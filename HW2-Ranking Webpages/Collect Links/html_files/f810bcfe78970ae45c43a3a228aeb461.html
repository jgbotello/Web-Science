As required by federal law The Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, 20 U.S.C 1092(f), known as the "Clery Act"), the university publishes an Annual Security and Fire Safety Report, which contains information regarding campus security, crime reporting polices, personal safety and crime prevention, and fire and crime statistics for our various campus locations.
