Our Office of Finance and Office of Student Financial Aid can help you explore ways to pay for college. By offering $200 million in financial aid packages annually, ODU sets you on a path for success from day one.  Tuition ODU charges a "per credit" tuition rate. There are additional fees and charges depending on academic standing, housing arrangements, and other factors.
