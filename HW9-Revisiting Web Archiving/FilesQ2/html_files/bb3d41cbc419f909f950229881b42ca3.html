Events are open to the public with free parking in designated areas. Many events are free, and tickets for select events are available at ODUArtsTix.com. We look forward to seeing you this season! The Baron and Ellin Gordon Art Galleries at Old Dominion University will present an art exhibition celebrating the life and work of Mary "Mama-Girl" Onley (1953 - 2018) in the fall of 2024.
