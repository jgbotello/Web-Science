MCC staff are available for brief consultations to review ODU’s application and admissions process, discuss military benefits (Tuition Assistance, VA Education Benefits, etc.) and address any questions you may have. We accept walk-ins and offer scheduled virtual, phone, and in-person consultations. To schedule, please complete the following contact form and someone from our office will reach out to you.
