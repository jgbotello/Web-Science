We are here to empower students and alumni in your career and leadership development from your first day as a Monarch through graduation and  your career, and we do so by collaborating strategically with campus and community partners. Key programs and services we offer Monarchs include career coaching and programs, leadership development, resume reviews and job search preparation, career fairs and employer connections, our Handshake job search database, student employment coordination and training, volunteer and service-learning programs, career treks, professional mentorship programs, living-learning and theme communities, and the Monarch Pantry.  Students Students Employers Employers Faculty & Staff Faculty & Staff Alumni Alumni Center for Career & Leadership Development Home About Us Students Prepare for Your Career Gain Experience Finding a Job Quinncia Employers Career Fairs ODU Career Summit Faculty & Staff Career Champions Classroom Presentations On-Campus Student Employment Letters of Reference Employer Referrals Alumni Events Contact Center for Career & Leadership Development 2202 Webb University Center Norfolk, VA 23529 757-683-4388 office ccld@odu.edu Directory Instagram linkedin youtube Upcoming Events January 28, 2025 Jan 28 An Evening with Dr. Bernice A.
