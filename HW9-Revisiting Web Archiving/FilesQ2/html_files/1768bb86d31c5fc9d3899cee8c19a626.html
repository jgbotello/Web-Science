Learn More Breadcrumb Home Regional Higher Education Centers Regional Higher Education Centers The Regional Higher Education Centers offer degree and certification programs so students in the localities of Virginia Beach, Hampton, Portsmouth, Suffolk, Chesapeake and beyond can meet their educational needs without traveling to the Norfolk campus. Our Centers also provide student success services to support timely degree completion, and quality space for continuing education and professional development activities.
