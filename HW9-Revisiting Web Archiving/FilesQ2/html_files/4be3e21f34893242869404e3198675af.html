PAWTUCKET Pawtucket-Central Falls Transit Center Terminal-Map_Pawtucket_Summer-2024 On Monday, January 23, 2023, RIPTA’s Pawtucket Transit Center moved from Roosevelt Avenue to Pine Street and Goff Avenue.  Routes 1, 71, 72, 73, 75, 76, 78, 80, QX and the R-Line will serve the new Pawtucket-Central Falls Transit Center where you can connect with MBTA Commuter Rail service to Boston and Providence. Route 78 and the R-Line will continue to serve Roosevelt Avenue.
