Explore Our Programs Breadcrumb Home School of Cybersecurity School of Cybersecurity The National Centers of Academic Excellence in Cybersecurity (NCAE-C) program managed by the National Security Agency (NSA) has designated Old Dominion University (ODU) as Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense (CAE-CD)  Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Operations (CAE-CO)  Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Research (CAE-R)    ODU has joined an extremely exclusive group of elite universities by becoming the 11th university to receive all three CAE designations since the NCAE-C program’s establishment in 1999.
