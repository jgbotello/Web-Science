How to Make a Good Presentation for a better grade There are numerous points that one should consider before making a presentation assignment. However, few of the important points that take a PPT beyond a certain threshold are: Too Much Text: If there is too much text, it means no one is going to pay attention to your presentation. It is like reading a book in front of the audience. Hence, never write more than 4-5 lines on one single slide. The font should be 24 Times New Roman. Use of Visuals: It is a scientifically proven fact that visuals help you remember things better. Hence, it should be employed while creating a presentation. People pay more attention to the PPTs with explanatory visuals including pictures and videos rather than just text. However, there should be some caution regarding the over usage of Pictures and videos. The presentation should be constructional and follow the proper approach. Organization of the Content: Content should make sense and coherent. For example, the first slide should provide an introduction rather than explaining important things about the topic of presentation. A presentation should be backed by the facts and figures. Labelling and numbering of diagrams and slides: It is a small thing that goes unnoticed. However, it is a good idea to put a number of every slide so that you can keep the track of time associated with your presentation.
