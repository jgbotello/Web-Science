About Academics Directory African American & African Studies (B.A./B.S.) The African American and African Studies (AAST) program offers the Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science degrees. About Academics Directory Asian Studies The Institute of Asian Studies initiates and coordinates the development and operation of special programs, and administers a minor and major in Asian Studies. About Academics Directory Communication & Theatre Arts View information for the department of Communication & Theatre arts at Old Dominion University About Academics Directory English Our degrees have majors and concentrations in a number of English fields, including Creative Writing, Linguistics, Technical Writing, Literature, Teacher Preparation, Rhetoric, and Technology and Media Studies. About Academics Directory Department of History The study of history is central to a liberal arts education, and provides students with the insights and analytical skills useful to understanding our contemporary world. The history major at Old Dominion University prepares students for careers in government service, law, international trade, education, journalism, and other areas. About Academics Directory Humanities The Institute for the Humanities at Old Dominion University offers an interdisciplinary Master of Arts degree in the Humanities. The program allows students to pursue individualized programs of study that incorporate classes from across the College of Arts and Letters. About Academics Directory Interdisciplinary Studies Program ODU offers degree programs for students who are interested in two or more fields of study that don't easily fit into a traditional college department: Cybersecurity, Leadership, Game Studies & Design, Professional Writing, General Engineering Technology, and more! About Academics Directory International Studies International Studies at Old Dominion University offers a range of bachelor's, master's, graduate certificates, and Ph.D. degrees that foster your interests and give you the skills to succeed professionally. About Academics Directory Graduate Program in International Studies (GPIS) Explore the Graduate Program in International Studies (GPIS) at Old Dominion University. About Academics Directory Diehn School of Music Outstanding musician-scholar-teachers provide a rich, challenging and creative environment where students may pursue their educational dreams and aspirations. The Diehn School of Music consists of award-winning faculty who are nationally and internationally recognized leaders in their fields. About Academics Directory Philosophy & Religious Studies The Department of Philosophy & Religious Studies offers a Bachelor of Arts degree in philosophy designed to give students a solid grounding in the historical development of philosophy, and an ability to analyze arguments proposed in serious discussions of any subject. About Academics Directory Political Science & Geography The Department of Political Science and Geography at Old Dominion University is a vibrant community of students, educators, scholars, practitioners, activists and engaged citizens. About Academics Directory Sociology & Criminal Justice The Department of Sociology and Criminal Justice at Old Dominion University features a diverse faculty with vast expertise in the areas of inequality, social justice, sociology of health, and criminology and criminal justice. About Academics Directory Department of Women's & Gender Studies A degree in Women's & Gender Studies provides a foundation to think critically, apply knowledge to a range of real world conditions, and develop a spoken and written voice that transfers into a range of opportunities. About Academics Directory World Languages & Cultures The Department of World Languages & Studies prepares students for today's globalized marketplace with foreign-language abilities and intercultural expertise.
